About Us

Welcome to Maple Glen Pizza, where we bring the taste of Italy to your neighborhood. We are passionate about crafting delicious pizzas using the finest ingredients.

Every pizza is made with love, from our hand-tossed dough to our signature sauce and the freshest toppings. We take pride in offering a variety of pizza options to satisfy every palate, from classic Margherita to innovative specialties.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond our pizzas. We source locally whenever possible, supporting our community and ensuring that every bite is a delightful experience. Whether you dine in, take out, or order for delivery, you can always expect exceptional service and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Thank you for being a part of our pizzeria family. We look forward to serving you the best pizza in town!
